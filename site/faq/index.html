Help Center

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for students, educators, and institutions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

Is In Institution free to use?
Yes! You can try the app today completely free. All core features — creating classes, running quizzes, publishing notes — are available at no cost. If you need an institution rollout with dedicated support, contact us.
Can I create a class and share notes?
Absolutely. Create a class, publish notes and resources, and keep everything organized in one place. Your students will be able to access notes directly from the class feed.
How does real-time quiz monitoring work?
When you run a live quiz, you can watch participation and performance as responses come in. See who's answered, track scores in real-time, and get instant insights into student engagement.
Can schools use this for exam scheduling?
Yes! Use our scheduling features to keep classes aligned with upcoming exams and timelines. Students and teachers stay on the same page with clear, organized schedules.
How do I invite students to my class?
Once you create a class, you can share a join link or code with your students. They can join directly from the app using your class code.
Is my data secure?
We take security seriously. We use secure authentication, encrypted connections, and follow best practices for data protection. See our Security page for more details.
How do I get help?
Email helpininstitutionapp@gmail.com for support or ininstitutionapp@gmail.com for general inquiries. We typically respond within 24-72 hours.
